# What's New in Version 1.3.2

**Released:** June 10, 2026

---

## ✨ New Features

### Product-specific purchase emails

Stripe purchases are now routed by product type so customers receive the right confirmation email and internal notifications stay accurate.

**What you can do:**
- **Support hours** — Customers get a support confirmation; no RMM agent or Datto setup
- **BNI Chapter Hub** — Customers get a Chapter Hub welcome with link to **hub.cloudigan.net**
- **RMM products** (Home Protect, Business, etc.) — Unchanged: Datto site, welcome email, Wix CMS

**How it works:**
1. Product name is classified automatically (RMM, support service, or BNI Chapter Hub)
2. Only the steps relevant to that product run
3. Admin notifications list what actually happened — no false Datto or Wix entries for non-RMM products

---

## 🔧 Improvements

### Clearer customer email copy

Confirmation emails describe what was purchased and what happens next — without mentioning unrelated products (RMM, Datto, etc.).

### BNI Chapter Hub branding

Chapter Hub emails use the **BNI Chapter Hub** name and **hub.cloudigan.net** for sign-in links.

### Reliable PM2 deployment

Production runs under PM2 on both nodes for consistent restarts and deploys.
